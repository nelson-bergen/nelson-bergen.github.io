About Me

Bergen Nelson

Recent Graduate, BS Mechanical Engineering

I've always enjoyed the process of building and solving problems. As a kid I spent countless hours in the wood shop with my dad and grandpa, and I fell in love with the type of mechanical thinking that goes into realizing a design in the physical world. I've continued to practice these skills in my personal life by repairing used bicycles, skis, and other outdoor equipment.

Now having completed two co-op experiences as Mechanical Design Co-op at EZ Ice Inc and Product R&D Co-op at Fresenius NxStage, I have been able to build my passion and skillset professionally, contributing to product design and development, sustaining efforts, failure investigations, and verification testing across both the sports equipment and medical device industries.

I really enjoyed the fulfilling nature of my work at my most recent co-op experience in the medical device industry at Fresenius NxStage. I hope to continue to develop my career in the medical device industry and work on products that improve patient outcomes and quality of life.

Outside of the lab, I am a cross country skier and triathlete. I also love to go to the climbing gym where I've been bouldering for a year and a half and I am currently pursuing my lead climb certification in order to start climbing big walls in the outdoors.
